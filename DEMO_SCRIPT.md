## The Story (say this out loud before touching the app)

"Infosys receives 500 engineering applications every week.
An AI reads them in 2 seconds each and decides: interview or reject.
Nobody programs discrimination into this AI. But it learned from
10 years of past hiring data — data where IIT graduates were
predominantly hired. So it learned a rule: IIT background is good,
state university background is not.

And because IITs admit only 7% SC/ST students versus 25% of the
Indian population, this AI is effectively filtering by caste —
without ever using the word. Silently. At scale. Every week.

FairLens makes this invisible injustice visible."
